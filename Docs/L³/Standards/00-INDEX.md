## Overview

L³ claims that every dimension has a scientific citation. These standards documents audit that claim and assess alignment with established psychometric instruments. Together they answer: "How well-supported is the L³ semantic space?"

## Summary

- **104 dimensions** across 8 groups (alpha through theta)
- **40+ unique citations** from neuroscience, psychology, information theory, semantics, and music theory
- **4 evidence levels**: Strong, Moderate, Theoretical, Pending
- **5 psychometric frameworks**: GEMS, Russell Circumplex, Osgood Semantic Differential, ITPRA (Huron), Berlyne Aesthetics
